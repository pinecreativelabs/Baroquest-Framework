Writhen Scroll

Inspiration

Writhen scroll was inspired by Solomonic columns, which are commonly found in Baroque furniture and architecture. The twisting animation mimicks the vertical movement found in these styles of decorative columns.

Ornate Scolling!

Fancy scroll animations also work with writhen scroll! This box uses the dash-in-right effect. Scroll up or down to see it dash into view. Other fancy scroll effects are added to other sections.

Customizeable

Custom content can be placed within each twist box! Style it however you want. The possibilities are limitless. This could be useful for image galleries!
